Super Smash Bros but with game realistic mechanics yipee! This took so long lmao

This is basically a project where you play Super Smash Bros with a select few retro game characters, but I tried to make it as accurate as possible to the actual classic games they're from. This was built on Godot, and certain sprites were created on Pixilart. 

Instructions:

WARNING:If you choose Link, due to me not coding him yet, he will not appear on-screen.

Best played with two people on one screen.

Controls:

Player 1: Arrow Keys with Shift and Comma for action keys.

Player 2:WASD with F and G for action keys. 



Super Mario specializes in jump attacks and sprinting, and uses powerups to prevent himself from dying.

Link isn't here yet.

Mega Man uses his versatile weaponry options to gain the edge on opponents.

Sonic the Hedgehog uses Spin Attacks(Spindash is unfortunately not working yet), as well as Shields to prevent himself from dying.

Warning:If your player falls off the platform, the best case scenario is just resetting the game.
